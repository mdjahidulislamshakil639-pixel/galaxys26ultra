§03 · Buyer's Guide

Everything You Need to Know Before Buying in Bangladesh.

Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is Samsung's 2026 flagship smartphone — refined, not reinvented. This complete buyer's guide covers every Galaxy S26 Ultra spec, every variant price in Bangladesh, every Dhaka retailer, EMI plans, warranty options, and decision factors that matter for BD buyers.

The Samsung Galaxy S26 Ultra is Samsung's flagship smartphone for 2026, announced at Galaxy Unpacked on February 25, 2026 and released globally on March 11, 2026. It sits at the top of the three-model S26 lineup alongside the standard S26 and S26+. While the Plus model saw a price increase, the Ultra keeps the same starting price as its predecessor at $1,299 internationally — roughly ৳1,89,999 officially in Bangladesh, or ৳1,55,000 for the UAE variant.

The S26 Ultra introduces a world-first Privacy Display — display-level technology (not a film) that narrows the viewing angle from the sides when enabled, making your screen appear dark to bystanders while remaining clear to you. Developed by Samsung Display under the codename "Flex Magic Pixel," it's the defining new feature of this generation.

Under the hood sits the Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 for Galaxy — Samsung's most powerful chip yet, used worldwide on the Ultra (the standard S26 and S26+ still use Exynos 2600 outside North America). The Ultra pairs it with 12GB of RAM standard, and 16GB on the 512GB and 1TB variants. Storage tops out at 1TB with no microSD expansion.

Camera-wise, Samsung kept the 200MP main sensor but opened the aperture from f/1.7 to f/1.4 — delivering 47% more light for dramatically better low-light photos. The 5× periscope telephoto also gets a wider f/2.9 aperture, using a new All Lens On Prism (ALoP) mechanism. Quad camera array: 200MP main + 50MP ultrawide + 10MP 3× + 50MP 5× periscope. Front: 12MP.

The design makes a notable material shift: after two generations with titanium, Samsung reverted to aluminum for 2026. It's lighter (214g vs 218g on S25 Ultra) and thinner at 7.9mm. Front and back protected by Gorilla Armor 2. Battery stays at 5000mAh but charging jumps to 60W wired — 0 to 75% in ~30 minutes. Wireless charging is 25W with reverse wireless support. The S Pen is still included.

Samsung promises 7 years of OS and security updates — meaning this phone will receive major Android versions through 2033. That matches Google's Pixel promise and beats most Android competitors.
